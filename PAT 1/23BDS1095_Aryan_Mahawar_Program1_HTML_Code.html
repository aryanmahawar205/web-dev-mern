Featured Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 100g pancetta, diced
  • Black pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy.
  3. Whisk eggs and mix with grated cheese.
  4. Drain spaghetti and combine with egg mixture.
  5. Add pancetta, season with pepper, and toss until coated.
